Editorial

Who is Jesus? Why follow him?

Jesus is God's son who was put on this earth to save humanity from sin and to spread the good news of God. We should follow Jesus so we can get a better understanding of God and to deepen our connection. Jesus also is the reason why he freed us from sin. The least thing we can do to pay him back is to listen to what he has to say.

Will Dalton

He Has Risen!

Mr. Dalton

Today we have a very special guest Jesus Christ! Thank you so much for being here to answer some questions. First question and properly a big one is why did you die for us?

Jesus Christ

I suffered on that cross for the people of the world so you all can be free from sin.

Mr. Dalton

Would you do that again if you needed to?

Jesus Christ

Of course I would do it a thousand times if it means I get to save the world from sin.

Mr. Dalton

What was your apostle's reaction when you walked by them after you have been resurrected?

Jesus Christ

They were completely shocked. All of them were lost for words but was glad to see me. I was very glad to see them as well.

Mr. Dalton

We only have time for one more question. When will you come again to save this world?

Jesus Christ

One of these days when the world needs me.

Mr. Dalton

That's all the time you heard it from him and thanks again for coming out

Will Dalton

Jo with Jesus

Mr. Beer

Welcome back to Jo with Jesus. I am Jack. Today we will be covering why people should follow Jesus.

Mr. Christ

Hello everyone. Yes, today we are going to cover all of your questions on why you should follow God and I.

Mr. Beer

Let's start simple. What is your mission? What do you want to accomplish?

Mr. Christ

My mission is simply to spread the good news of God, and his love.

Mr. Beer

Sounds like quite a daunting task. How are you able to deel with all the stress that must you must have? Who are you as a leader?

Mr. Christ

I am God's son. I am both human and divine which allows me to lead as the Messiah. I have spent my earthly life guiding humanity to a path of redemption and love.

Mr. Beer

Profound. Make sure to check back tomorrow for another round of Jo with Jesus.

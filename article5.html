Forgiveness and Crucifixion, the Death of Jesus

Luke 23: 33-34 describes Jesus on the hill of Calvary, during his crucifixion as he mutters the divine words “Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do.” Through this saying Jesus promotes God’s unconditional love and forgiveness. Furthermore, Jesus demonstrates both his divinity and human likeness through this horrible event.

Jesus' Feast with Friends

On Jesus' last meal, he shares his passion for God with his disciples by breaking the bread and drinking the wine, which represents his body and blood. In Luke 22:7-38 Jesus shared his passion for God when he broke the bread and shared the wine from a cup. These signified Jesus on body and blood and are seen in mass around the world today. The symbols of Jesus' body and blood are seen in Christians Eucharist which is one of the most important things in the church. Jesus breaking the bread and sharing the wine shows Jesus passion and love for his followers.

Jesus Suprise Resurrection

Jesus surprised his followers when they found his tomb empty and later found him walking around. In Luke 24:1-12 Jesus surprised the women when they went to take care of the tomb and found it empty. “They found the stone rolled away from the tomb, they did not find the body of the Lord”(Luke 24:2:3). Later in Luke 24:36-49 Jesus appears to his disciples and surprises them. Jesus' resurrection shows his power and him coming back to his loyal friends is why you should follow him.
